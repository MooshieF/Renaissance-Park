Renaissance Park

How yesterday's garbage became today's greenspace, and Fresh Kills Landfill on Staten Island was reborn as Freshkills Park.

Early Dutch settlers named the area Fresh Kills, which means fresh water. It was once a beautiful meadow, until it became a dumping ground for New York City's garbage. Now, its beauty will be restored.
